Welcome to Radiotation!

Here's how it works

  1. Join an existing room with your friends or create a new one.
  2. Search for your favorite songs, and add them to your playlist.
  3. Open up the Radiotation app for Android and start playing it back.

Radiotation will handle the rest, giving everyone equal playtime in the car (as long as everyone has added music!)
