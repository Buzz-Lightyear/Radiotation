Here's how it works

  1. Log in below.
  2. Players have icons indicating stuff about them, hover over them to see what they mean.
  3. Each round will begin with a normal round of Texas Hold 'Em, where you can do shit like bet, fold, check, call, and all that other good stuff.
  4. The winner(s) of the round will be dealt a card from their hand or the community cards, that becomes their card in Kings (Rules to the right)
  5. There's also a chug option that gets more likely every round, you'll know it when you see it.
  6. When we're all sick of the game or everyone is out, we stop playing, and the person with the most money/last Airbender™ gets $250, cash.
  7. If Pat Coffey wins, he gets nothing because he's 2,500 miles away.

Slightly Different than usual Kings Rules

  • Ace: Waterfall Every player begins drinking, and no one can stop until the player before them does
  • 2: You Whoever drew the card assigns a drink
  • 3: Me Whoever drew the card drinks
  • 4: Floor Everyone races to touch the floor, last person to do so drinks
  • 5: Guys All guys drink
  • 6: Chicks Well...uh, we all drink again
  • 7: Heaven All players point towards the sky, last player to do so drinks
  • 8: Mate Pick a person to drink with, there can be chains
  • 9: Rhyme Say a phrase, and everyone else must say phrases that rhyme
  • 10: Categories Pick a category, and say something from that category
  • Jack: Never have I ever With three fingers, starts with the person who drew the card
  • Queen: Questions Normal Question Master Shizz
  • King: Ruler Make a rule that everyone must follow until the next King is drawn
